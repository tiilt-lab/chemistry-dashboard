Reviewing keywords and questions

You can monitor each pod's discussion during recording or after recording has finished to see a timeline, the direction of discussion, detected keywords, and discussion features.

  1. Using your computer or tablet, navigate to the Discussion Capture server and sign in.

  2. Click the Discussions button on the main Dashboard page:

  3. On the Manage Discussions page, click the current discussion (indicated by a red microphone) to view an in-progress discussion, or click a previous discussion in the list.

  4. The Overview page displays a tile for each pod that was selected as a recording source. Click a tile to view discussion data from that pod.

    If students need assistance during a session, they can press the button on the top of the pod. After the button is pressed, the pod's icon will flash on the Overview page. Press the button again to turn off the call for help.

    Adjusting the display time window

    To adjust the timeframe you want to review, use the timeline control at the top of the page.

    For example, drag the handles to the Start and End marks to view all discussion activity. To focus on a smaller section of the discussion, drag the handles in, and drag the bar to move the timeline window.

    Reviewing the discussion timeline

    The discussion timeline shows discussion events from the selected timeframe. Silence is displayed in white, discussion events are displayed in green, and questions are displayed in orange.

    Click any event to display a transcription of the event in a pop-up window. If you want to see the event in a broader context, click the View All button in the window to display the full transcript.

    Reviewing the discussion direction

    The discussion direction graph shows the distribution of discussion within the selected timeframe.

    Front corresponds to the 0° mark on the pod and indicates the front of the room. In the following sample, we can see that students on the students on the sides and back of the table participated in the discussion and that the students on the right side contributed the most.

    To adjust the graph, click and choose Show Tools from the menu. Drag the Zones slider to adjust the number of divisions in the graph, and drag the Offset slider to adjust the alignment of the zones.

    Please note that Discussion direction is not available when"Joining a discussion with a student device."

    Reviewing detected keywords

    The keyword detection list shows the keywords that were detected within the selected timeframe.

    Click any keyword to display a transcription where that keyword was detected.

    To see a timeline view of the keywords, click and choose Show Timeline from the menu.

    Reviewing discussion features

    The discussion features list displays qualities about the discussion within the selected timeframe. These qualities relate to the emotional tone, use of analytic reasoning, and clout. You can use these qualities to evaluate whether students are able to speak confidently about the topic or whether they are confused.
