Naming and locating pods in the Dashboard

When you have multiple discussion tables in your room, you can rename each pod to help you identify it easily. You can also use the Dashboard to toggle a pod's lights to help you locate it in the room.

Naming a pod

  1. Using your computer or tablet, navigate to the Discussion Capture server and sign in.

  2. Click the Pods button on the main Dashboard page:

  3. On the Manage Pods page, click the button on a pod and then click Rename. A dialog box is displayed.

  4. Type a new name in the box, and then click Rename.

Locating a pod

  1. Using your computer or tablet, navigate to the Discussion Capture server and sign in.

  2. Click the Pods button on the main Dashboard page:

  3. On the Manage Pods page, click the light icon for a pod.

    The lights on the top of the pod turn on temporarily so you can locate the named pod in your room.
